# Data availability

The source code, experiment definitions, split logic, manuscript source, and small derived result tables are included here. The preserved historical prediction files and their SHA-256 manifest are included so the artifact-based statistical family can be audited exactly. The raw sensor recordings, intermediate signal caches, trained checkpoints, and profilometer data are not mirrored in Git because of their size. Research-use access to the available large data artifacts may be requested from the corresponding author.
